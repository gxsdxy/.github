## 📖 关于 · About

你好！我是广西水利电力职业技术学院的一名计算机教师。
这里存放我在教学中使用的课件、示例代码和练习题，欢迎学生和同行取用。

Hi! I'm a computer science teacher at Guangxi Vocational College of Water
Resources and Electric Power. This is where I keep the courseware, sample
code, and exercises used in my classes — students and fellow teachers are
welcome to use them.

  Each course has its own repository — find it by course name.
